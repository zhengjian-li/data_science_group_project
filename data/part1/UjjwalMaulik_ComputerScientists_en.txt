Ujjwal Maulik is an Indian Computer Scientist and a Professor. He is the former chair of the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Jadavpur University, Kolkata, West Bengal, India. He also held the position of the principal-in-charge and the head of the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Kalyani Government Engineering College. He has worked in many countries including India, US, Germany, France, Australia, China, Italy, Poland. Slovenia and Hungary.
