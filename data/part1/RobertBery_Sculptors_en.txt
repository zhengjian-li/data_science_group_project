Robert Bery is a Manhattan-based artist working in a variety of mediums, including painting, photography and sculpture; his work in many public and private collections. These include those of the Federal Reserve Bank, the Smithsonian, Alec Baldwin, Benjamin Bratt, David Bowie, Willem Dafoe,  Alan Dershowitz, and the Clintons. Bery was the Lead Plaintiff in the successful Second Circuit court "Bery vs. City of New York" First Amendment case.
